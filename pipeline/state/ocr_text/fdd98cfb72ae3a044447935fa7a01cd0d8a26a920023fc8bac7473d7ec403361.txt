Job Description

ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES

-Develops lesson plans and instructional materials and provides individualized and small group instruction in order to
adapt the curriculum to the needs of each pupil.

-Teaches reading, language arts, social studies, and/or other assigned subject areas to pupils in a classroom u
concepts and pedagogy adopted by the Board and other appropriate learning activities.

ng

-Evaluates pupils’ academic and social growth, keeps appropriate records, and prepares progress reports in a timely
manner.

-Collaborate with other 5th grade teachers, including the Special Education Teacher.

-Communicates with parents through conferences and other means to discuss pupil’s progress and interpret the
school program.

-Identifies pupil needs and cooperates with other professional staff members in assessing and helping pupils solve
health, attitude and learning problems.

-Creates an effective environment for learning through functional and attractive displays, bulletin boards, and
interest centers.

-Creates an environment of respect and rapport, and establishing a culture for learning in which safety and order is
the norm.

-Maintains professional competence through in-service education activities provided by the district and/or self-
selected professional growth activities.

-Maintains accurate, complete, and correct records as required by law, district policy, and administrative regulation.

-Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the Common Core State Standards, effective instructional strategies,
classroom management, learning assessment and diagnosis, and research related to learning.

-Other duties as assigned by administration.
